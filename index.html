AI Helps Clean Oceans

Artificial intelligence is playing a major role in protecting marine life. Smart robots and drones are now being used to locate and collect plastic waste from the oceans. These AI-powered machines can identify debris, map pollution areas, and even predict where new waste will appear. Experts believe this technology could drastically reduce ocean pollution within the next 20 years.

Mars Mission Success

NASA has successfully completed its latest mission to Mars, marking a new era in space exploration. The robotic lander collected rock samples and sent high-resolution images of the Martian surface back to Earth. Scientists say the mission brings humanity closer to understanding whether life once existed on the Red Planet. The data will also help prepare for future manned missions to Mars in the coming decades.
